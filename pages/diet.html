Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa (rinsed)
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley or cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese (optional, omit for vegan)
  • 1/4 cup cooked chickpeas (optional, for added protein)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Dressing:

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar or apple cider vinegar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the rinsed quinoa and water (or vegetable broth). Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15 minutes or until the water is absorbed and the quinoa is tender. Fluff with a fork and let it cool.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, vinegar, minced garlic, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper. Adjust the seasoning to taste.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, red onion, and avocado. If using, add the feta cheese and chickpeas.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley or cilantro. Serve the salad chilled or at room temperature.

Zucchini Noodles with Pesto (Low-Carb, Gluten-Free, Vegan)

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium zucchinis
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts or walnuts
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast (for a cheesy flavor)
  • 1 clove garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Make the Zucchini Noodles:
    • Use a spiralizer or vegetable peeler to create zucchini noodles (zoodles). Set aside.
  2. Prepare the Pesto:
    • In a food processor, combine basil leaves, pine nuts, nutritional yeast, garlic, salt, and pepper. While processing, slowly add olive oil until the mixture is smooth and creamy.
  3. Combine:
    • Toss the zucchini noodles with the pesto sauce until well-coated.
  4. Serve:
    • Garnish with cherry tomatoes and serve immediately. You can enjoy this dish raw or lightly saut√© the zoodles in a pan for a warm dish.

Chia Pudding (Vegan, Gluten-Free, Low-Calorie)

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk (or any plant-based milk)
  • 1-2 tablespoons maple syrup or honey (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Fresh fruits, nuts, or seeds for topping

Instructions:

  1. Mix Ingredients:
    • In a jar or bowl, combine chia seeds, almond milk, maple syrup or honey, and vanilla extract. Stir well to prevent clumping.
  2. Refrigerate:
    • Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight. The chia seeds will absorb the liquid and form a pudding-like consistency.
  3. Serve:
    • Stir the pudding once more before serving. Top with fresh fruits like berries, sliced bananas, nuts, or seeds for added texture and flavor.

Cauliflower Rice Stir-Fry (Low-Carb, Gluten-Free, Vegan Option)

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium head of cauliflower, grated or processed into rice-sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce or tamari (gluten-free)
  • 1 egg, beaten (optional, omit for vegan)
  • Green onions or cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Cauliflower Rice:
    • Grate the cauliflower using a box grater or pulse in a food processor until it resembles rice.
  2. Cook Vegetables:
    • In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion, bell pepper, carrot, and garlic. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Cook the Cauliflower Rice:
    • Add the cauliflower rice to the pan, stirring well to combine. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the cauliflower is tender but not mushy.
  4. Add Peas and Soy Sauce:
    • Stir in the peas and soy sauce, cooking for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Optional Egg:
    • If using, push the cauliflower rice to one side of the pan and pour the beaten egg into the empty side. Scramble until cooked, then mix it into the cauliflower rice.
  6. Serve:
    • Garnish with green onions or cilantro and serve hot

Greek Yogurt Parfait (High-Protein, Low-Sugar)

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup granola (choose a low-sugar option)
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped nuts or seeds (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a glass or bowl, layer Greek yogurt, granola, and mixed berries.
  2. Drizzle honey or maple syrup over the top for extra sweetness.
  3. Sprinkle with chopped nuts or seeds for added crunch and serve immediately.

Lentil Soup (High-Protein, Vegan, Gluten-Free)

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried lentils, rinsed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley or cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion, carrots, and celery. Cook until softened.
  2. Stir in garlic, cumin, and turmeric. Cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the lentils, vegetable broth, and diced tomatoes (if using). Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 25-30 minutes until the lentils are tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley or cilantro and serve hot.
