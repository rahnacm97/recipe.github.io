Black Tea

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon of loose black tea leaves (or 1 black tea bag)
  • Sugar or honey (optional, to taste)
  • Lemon slice (optional, for flavor)

Instructions:

  1. Start by boiling 1 cup of water in a kettle or small pot.
  2. Once the water reaches a rolling boil, turn off the heat and add 1 teaspoon of black tea leaves or place a black tea bag in your cup.
  3. Let the tea steep for 3-5 minutes, depending on how strong you like it. The longer you steep, the stronger the tea will be.
  4. If you like your tea sweet, add sugar or honey to taste while the tea is still hot. Stir well to dissolve.
  5. For a refreshing twist, add a slice of lemon to the tea. This also adds a slight tanginess.(Optional)
  6. Pour the tea into your cup if you used loose tea leaves and strained it. If you used a tea bag, simply remove it after steeping.

Coffee

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of water
  • 1-2 teaspoons of coffee powder (instant coffee or ground coffee)
  • 1/2 to 1 cup of milk (optional, depending on your preference)
  • 1-2 teaspoons of sugar (optional, to taste)

Instructions:

  1. Start by boiling 1 cup of water in a kettle or small pot.
  2. Once the water boils, turn off the heat. Add 1-2 teaspoons of coffee powder to your cup, depending on how strong you like your coffee.
  3. Add sugar to taste and stir well to dissolve.
  4. If you prefer black coffee, skip this step. For a milder, creamy coffee, add 1/2 to 1 cup of hot milk. You can adjust the milk quantity based on how strong or light you like your coffee.
  5. Stir well to combine all the ingredients. Serve hot.

Tea

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon of black tea leaves (or 1 black tea bag)
  • 1/2 cup of milk (any type: whole, skimmed, or plant-based)
  • 1-2 teaspoons of sugar (optional, to taste)
  • A pinch of cardamom or a small piece of ginger (optional, for extra flavor)

Instructions:

  1. Start by boiling 1 cup of water in a kettle or small pot.
  2. Once the water is boiling, add 1 teaspoon of black tea leaves or a tea bag. Allow it to boil for 1-2 minutes to release the flavor.
  3. Pour in 1/2 cup of milk. Stir the mixture and bring it back to a boil.
  4. Add 1-2 teaspoons of sugar to taste. Stir well to ensure the sugar dissolves completely.
  5. Reduce the heat and let the tea simmer for 2-3 minutes. If you prefer a stronger tea, you can let it simmer a little longer.
  6. For a spiced tea, you can add a pinch of cardamom or a small piece of ginger while the tea is simmering. (Optional)
  7. Strain the tea into a cup to remove the tea leaves (or remove the tea bag). Serve hot.

Watermelon Juice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of watermelon (deseeded and cubed)
  • A pinch of salt
  • A squeeze of lemon juice (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Blend the watermelon cubes in a blender until smooth.
  2. Strain the juice if you prefer it without pulp.
  3. Add a pinch of salt and a squeeze of lemon juice for extra flavor.
  4. Serve chilled.

Orange Juice

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 fresh oranges
  • Sugar or honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cut the oranges in half and squeeze the juice using a hand juicer or an electric juicer.
  2. Strain the juice to remove any pulp or seeds, if desired.
  3. Add sugar or honey if you like it sweeter.
  4. Serve chilled with ice cubes.

Carrot Juice

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 medium-sized carrots
  • 1 apple (optional, for sweetness)
  • 1 inch of ginger (optional, for a kick)
  • Lemon juice to taste

Instructions:

  1. Peel and chop the carrots and apple (if using).
  2. Blend the carrots, apple, and ginger in a blender until smooth.
  3. Strain the juice if needed.
  4. Add lemon juice to taste and stir well.
  5. Serve immediately.

Pineapple Juice

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe pineapple (peeled and chopped)
  • 1-2 teaspoons of sugar or honey (optional)
  • A few mint leaves (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Blend the pineapple chunks in a blender until smooth.
  2. Strain the juice to remove the pulp if you prefer a smoother texture.
  3. Add sugar or honey if you want it sweeter.
  4. Garnish with mint leaves and serve chilled.

Mixed Fruit Juice

Ingredients:

  • 1 apple, chopped
  • 1 orange, peeled
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup water or coconut water

Instructions:

  1. Blend all the fruits together with water or coconut water until smooth.
  2. Strain if you prefer a smoother juice.
  3. Serve immediately, chilled or over ice.

Lemonade

Ingredients:

  • 2 lemons
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar or honey
  • 2 cups of cold water
  • Ice cubes
  • Mint leaves (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Squeeze the juice from the lemons and pour it into a jug.
  2. Add the sugar or honey and stir until dissolved.
  3. Add cold water and mix well.
  4. Serve over ice with a few mint leaves for garnish.
